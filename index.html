What is the
Digital Divide?

The gap that exists between those with ready access to the internet
or technology and those without.

The Issue

What is the root of the problem?

The digital divide is rooted in socio-economic differences. Simply put, technology access is expensive and underserved communities where income is held constant have less access to the internet. It also relates to location, race, and ethnicity as economic status and public school funding has a correlation to those factors.
