What are some ways to close the gap?

- Provide students and parents with take-home technologies.

- Improve students’ access to technology, and the quality of that access, in their classrooms and schools

Steps To Improve Schools

1. Ensure that teachers have adequate technical support

2. Make sure that teachers have the hardware and software they need for the instructional technology approaches they will be trained to use.

3. Focus training on how to integrate technology into instruction, not just on the technology itself.

4. Focus the training on tools for language development and problem solving.

5. Focus the training on how to use the tools to create a product.

6. Train teachers on how to select, find and evaluate software and web sites.

7. Inspire teachers to engage in telecollaborative projects via email, the web, and video conferencing.

8. Team up teachers during and between workshops.

9. Provide follow-up training support to teachers after workshops.

10. Assess their instructional technology programs, focusing the evaluation on issues of both excellence and equity.
